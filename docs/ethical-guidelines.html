Ethical Guidelines

Core Ethical Principles

1. Do No Harm

Our primary responsibility is to ensure our actions do not cause harm to individuals, organizations, or society.

2. Respect Privacy

We respect and protect the privacy rights of all individuals and organizations.

3. Transparency

We maintain transparency in our methods and intentions while respecting necessary confidentiality.

4. Accountability

We take responsibility for our actions and their consequences.

5. Continuous Learning

We commit to ongoing education and improvement of our ethical understanding and practices.

Professional Conduct

Client Relations

  • Clear communication of scope and limitations
  • Honest assessment of capabilities
  • Protection of client confidentiality

Technical Operations

  • Documentation of all activities
  • Minimization of system impact
  • Proper handling of discovered vulnerabilities

Reporting

  • Accurate and complete documentation
  • Clear communication of findings
  • Actionable recommendations
