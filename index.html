Frequently Asked Questions

I'm law enforcement and I need assistance, what do I do?

When joining the server. You can choose a "law enforcemen" role and granted access to ask for assistanc or you may direct message @sirhaxalot or a moderator directly, from here a private channel will be created for you with restricted access for discussion. We will also need to verify your law enforcement status with an email from your work address or other suitable method. A list of mods is available in the announcement channel.

I'm an ethical hacker, how can I help?

We will post for assistance when requested by Law Enforcement in the general channel if you are selected you will be given access to a private channel where you can assist. You may also need to sign an NDA or Limited Access agreement. It is also highly recommended that you reach out to your local law enforcement if you have an existing relationship with them to let them know we are here.

Can I have some information about training?

Free training will be given, announcements will be made in the #training channel.

What's the legality of this?

You will not be exposed to any illegal material so no laws will be broken.

I'm new to ethical hacking, can I join?

Yes - all are welcome and free training will be provided.

What sort of assistance will this community provide law enforcement?

Whatever they need within the scope of the law.
