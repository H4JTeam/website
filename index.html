Hackers 4 Justice

Hackers4Justice is a global volunteer organization composed of ethical hackers, DFIR & OSINT specialists, and software developers who aim to assist law enforcement and humanitarian causes using digital skills.

Join Our Network

Mission Statement

Our mission is to support justice, protect vulnerable communities, and assist law enforcement agencies through ethical, legal, and secure cyber activities.

DFIR & OSINT Specialists

Collecting open-source intelligence (OSINT) from public sources and analyzing digital evidence.

Ethical Hackers

Running honeypots or research systems on their own infrastructure to monitor malicious behavior legally.

Software Developers

Developing tools or frameworks that assist in crime prevention or threat analysis.
